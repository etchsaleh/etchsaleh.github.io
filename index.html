About Me

I spent the first 8 years of my life in Kuwait, after which I moved to my hometown, Alexandria. I attended the British School of Alexandria in 2014 and I graduated from the Arab Academy for Science and Technology in January 2020, with a B.S (Hons) in Computer Engineering.
Throughout the last couple of years, I became well versed in object oriented programming and problem solving, having interned at german software company in 2017 called POET, as well as interning in 2019 at American Express in the UK.
Moreover, I am very keen on graphic design and UI/UX development, designing a vast array of logos for clients around the world, as well as working on several application GUIs.
